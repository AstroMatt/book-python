Strategy
========


Rationale
---------
* EN: Strategy
* PL: Strategia
* Type: object


Use Cases
---------
* Store images with compressor and filters


Design
------
* Similar to `State Pattern`
* No single states
* Can have multiple states
* Different behaviors are represented by strategy objects
